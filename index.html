About

A Journey of Transformation

With over two decades of dedicated practice and teaching, Giri Raj Timshina has become a beacon of wellness and spiritual guidance in the international community.

My spiritual journey began in the sacred city of Varanasi in 1998, where I immersed myself in the profound traditions of Yoga, Meditation, Tantra, Mantra, and Reiki. The ancient wisdom of this holy city became the foundation of my practice, shaping my understanding of the interconnectedness of body, mind, and spirit.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of sharing these transformative practices across Nepal, India, and Vietnam, working with diverse communities and individuals seeking balance, healing, and inner peace. My approach combines traditional techniques with contemporary understanding, making ancient wisdom accessible to modern wellness seekers.

Today, based in Da Nang, Vietnam, I continue to serve as a bridge between Eastern spiritual traditions and the global wellness community, bringing authentic practices to luxury resorts and international clients who value both excellence and authenticity.
