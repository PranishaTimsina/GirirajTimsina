About

A Journey of Transformation

With over two decades of dedicated practice and teaching, Giri Raj Timshina has become a beacon of wellness and spiritual guidance in the international community.

My spiritual journey began in the sacred city of Varanasi in 1998, where I immersed myself in the profound traditions of Yoga, Meditation, Tantra, Mantra, and Reiki. The ancient wisdom of this holy city became the foundation of my practice, shaping my understanding of the interconnectedness of body, mind, and spirit.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of sharing these transformative practices across Nepal, India, and Vietnam, working with diverse communities and individuals seeking balance, healing, and inner peace. My approach combines traditional techniques with contemporary understanding, making ancient wisdom accessible to modern wellness seekers.

Today, based in Da Nang, Vietnam, I continue to serve as a bridge between Eastern spiritual traditions and the global wellness community, bringing authentic practices to luxury resorts and international clients who value both excellence and authenticity.

Don't know how to communicate this effectively enough, but all I can say is that the world has a mysterious way of placing you in the right place at the right time.

Little over two months ago I had no idea I would have been in Vietnam evaluating who I have become, how the last two years have played out, and how I wish to live a life of remarkable impact.

2017 has been a rip snorter of a year, and it's thrown more curveballs than ever before. There's been some amazing business and personal successes; and some pretty devastating cases of pain, heartache and failure.

My most active and personal endeavour this year was a simple one: limiting the negative impact that my ego has upon my life through a more spiritual existence. I made progress, but I definitely have plenty of work to do to get it all dialled in.

However, it all got a little too much, and my awesome parents saw a need for me to rest up. They knew I needed to get away, so they assisted me in getting to the most serene part of Da Nang to rejuvenate and get better.

However, the highlight of my journey this year wasn't eating Banh Mi in Hoi An nor getting on the Channel 7 News for @owenandedwin; it was meeting this remarkable man who I now call my friend.

Geeri, the Yoga Master at the Naman Retreat, has done a lot more than just run a yoga and meditation class for me - he's shown me an amazing philosophy how to live a life of Joy, Truth and Unconditional Love. He's shown me the beauty in non-attachment, loving for love's sake, and living a life free of expectations.

If it wasn't for all that has happened, we wouldn't have met in the most remarkable of circumstances. Is it the universe conspiring or is it merely a coincidence that all of this has come about? Who truly knows? One thing, that's for sure, is that the magic is ready to weave itself into your own fabric. However, you oughta see the omens and be ready to receive them.
