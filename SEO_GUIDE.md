## 4. **Sitemap.xml**

### What Created:
A complete XML sitemap listing all pages and sections of your website with:
- Priority levels (important pages get higher priority)
- Change frequency
- Last modification dates

### Why It Matters:
- Helps Google crawl and index all your pages efficiently
- Submitting to Google Search Console accelerates indexing
- Tells Google which pages are most important

---

## 5. **Robots.txt**

### What Created:
A robots.txt file that:
- Allows all content for indexing
- Points to your sitemap
- Sets crawl delays (respectful to servers)

### Why It Matters:
- Guides search engine bots on how to crawl your site
- Prevents indexing of private content
- Improves crawl efficiency
