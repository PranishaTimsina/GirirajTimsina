## ğŸ’¡ Important Tips

1. **Don't keyword stuff**
   - Bad: "Giri Raj Timshina yoga Giri Raj Timshina meditation Giri Raj Timshina..."
   - Good: "Giri Raj Timshina teaches yoga and meditation with expertise"

2. **Write naturally**
   - Don't write for Google, write for humans
   - Google will rank good human content

3. **Use your name**
   - Google needs to connect content to your name
   - Use "Giri Raj Timshina" (full name) not just "Giri"

4. **Add images**
   - Images with alt text help SEO
   - Alt text example: "Giri Raj Timshina teaching Hatha Yoga class"

5. **Add links**
   - Link to your main website
   - Links tell Google your content is related

## ğŸ“ Content Writing Tips

### Structure of a Good Blog Post:

1. **Title** (Include your name)
   - "The Complete Guide to Hatha Yoga with Giri Raj Timshina"

2. **Introduction** (What and Why)
   - "In this guide, Giri Raj Timshina explains..."

3. **Body** (How and Details)
   - 3-5 main sections
   - Each section 100-200 words
   - Natural mention of your name

4. **Examples** (Real Stories)
   - "One of Giri Raj Timshina's students..."

5. **Call-to-Action** (What's next)
   - "Schedule your session with Giri Raj Timshina"
   - Link to your contact form

6. **Link Back** (Internal Link)
   - "Learn more about my services"
   - "Book with Giri Raj Timshina"
